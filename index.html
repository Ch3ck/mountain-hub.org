Who We Are

Mountain Tech Hub is a co-production space for creatives, developers, phreaks & organizers in Buea, Cameroon;
which aims at building a productive techosystem where hardware and software marry to give birth to cool things
that matter.

How We Operate

Located in the heart of Buea,
we are early stage creatives and hackers dedicated to the mastery of our craft with support and resources to push the boundaries of innovation and long-term growth.

Our Techosystem

We're a registered organization within the Fako Division of the South West Region. We're not an incubator.
If you're looking for an incubator in Cameroon, please visit www.activspaces.com. We're always open for Meet-ups, Hackathons, Code Labs, etc; everything which can inspire youths to innovate and of course, make the world a better place!
